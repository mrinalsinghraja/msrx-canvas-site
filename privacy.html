Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 25, 2026  ·  MSRX Canvas

Overview

MSRX Canvas is developed by Mrinal Singh Raja (MSRX). MSRX Canvas does not collect, transmit, sell, share, or track personal data. It contains no analytics, no advertising, and no account system.

Data We Collect

None. MSRX Canvas does not collect any personal information. There are no accounts, no sign-in, no usage analytics, and no advertising identifiers.

Your Content Stays on Your Mac

Documents and images you edit in MSRX Canvas remain on your Mac unless you explicitly save, export, share, or move them yourself. App preferences and your list of recently opened files are stored locally on your device using macOS UserDefaults and security-scoped bookmarks.

AI Assist

AI Assist is included and requires no account or API key. It runs entirely on your Mac: image perception uses Apple's Vision framework and text generation uses Apple Foundation Models (Apple Intelligence).

Your canvas is never uploaded, transmitted, stored, or sent to any server or third party. Everything is processed on-device by Apple Intelligence, which is part of macOS. Generative AI features require Apple Intelligence (macOS 26 on Apple Silicon).

Third-Party Services

  • Apple App Store — for app distribution.

MSRX Canvas makes no network connections at all and bundles no third-party tracking or analytics SDKs.

Children's Privacy

MSRX Canvas is rated 4+ and is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy. Significant changes will be reflected by updating the Effective Date above.
